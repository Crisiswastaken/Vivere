02 — What it senses

Three senses. One intention.

Vivere listens to the page the way a host listens to a room — adjusting light, tone, and posture before a guest ever articulates a need.

i.

Attention

Heatmaps of where eyes linger, where cursors wander, where interest fades. Every glance becomes a quiet vote on what survives the next rewrite.

ii.

Friction

Every micro-stall, every abandoned field, every confused hover is logged as a small wound. Vivere heals them before the next visitor arrives.

iii.

Resonance

Copy variants are quietly tested against one another in real time — no setup, no split-screen. The page finds its own voice by listening to the room.

04 — The method

Four movements, endlessly looping.

01

Listen

Every signal — cursor, scroll, hover, hesitation — becomes a data point in a continuous portrait of your audience.

02

Interpret

Vivere weighs signals against intent. It distinguishes confusion from contemplation, indifference from restraint.

03

Reshape

Headlines, order, hierarchy, and tone are rewritten in measured strokes — never reckless, always reversible.

04

Verify

Every change is held accountable to the metrics that matter. If it doesn't lift the page, it doesn't stay.
